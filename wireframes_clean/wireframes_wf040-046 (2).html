43 · About page
← About PureTask

Cleaning, made trustworthy.

PureTask connects Northern California homes with verified independent cleaners — backed by GPS check-in, photo proof, and pay-on-approval.

Why we built this
Booking a cleaner means letting a stranger into your home. The existing options either ask you to take it on faith or hide the work behind opaque rules and fine print. We thought there was room for something honest — a marketplace where every cleaning is documented in real time, where cleaners keep most of what they earn, and where pricing has no hidden lines.
How we're different
Real photo proof
Every required room photographed before and after. You see exactly what was done.
GPS-verified arrivals
Cleaners check in via geo-fence. You know when they arrive and when they leave.
Pay only after you approve
Payment held in escrow until you review the photos. 24-hour auto-approval if you're satisfied.
Cleaners keep 85–90%
Lowest commission of any major cleaning marketplace. Better pay attracts better cleaners.
Where we operate
Northern California, from San Francisco to Reno. Sacramento is our home metro and our largest cleaner network.

BOOKING & PAYMENT
When am I charged? −
Payment is authorized when you book but only captured after you approve the cleaning. If a cleaning is auto-approved at the 24-hour mark, payment is released to the cleaner then. You're never charged before the work is done.

TRUST & SAFETY
Are cleaners background checked? −
Yes. Every cleaner passes a Checkr background check (national criminal database, sex offender registry, identity verification) before they can take bookings. Checks renew annually.
