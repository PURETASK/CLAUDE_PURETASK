Common questions
How does the tier system work?
Your reliability score determines your tier. Higher tier = lower platform fee + higher rate range. Drops are slow (14+ days of low performance) and you can appeal.
Am I an employee?
No. You're an independent contractor — you set your rates, schedule, and how you work. We provide the booking platform and tools.
Do I need insurance?
Strongly recommended, not required. Cleaners with insurance get a verified badge and stand out to customers.
When do I get paid?
Every Friday at noon, free. Or instantly after each job approved by the customer for a 5% fee.
